The Problem

Only ~60% of orders arrive on time.

In the North and Northeast regions,
average delivery runs 10+ days late.

And late delivery is the #1 predictor of a 1-star review.

Key finding: Delivery delay is almost entirely a geography problem. SP sellers shipping to SP customers are early. Everything else degrades with distance.

The Forgiveness Window

Reviews stay stable for delays up to ~7 days.
Beyond that, mean review score drops sharply from 4.2 → 2.8.

Before the window (≤7 days late)

  • Mean review: 4.1–4.3
  • Customers still forgiving
  • Proactive communication helps

After the window (>7 days late)

  • Mean review: 2.8–3.2
  • Score recovery very unlikely
  • Prevention is the only strategy

→ Operational threshold: flag any order predicted >7 days late for immediate seller alert

Angle 2 — Customer Segments

💚 Loyal

⚠️ At-Risk

Good history · lapsing recently
Action: Win-back campaign, personalised offer

Long inactive · low engagement
